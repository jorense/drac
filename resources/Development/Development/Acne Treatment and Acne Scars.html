Acne Treatment and Acne Scars

  • Pimple Injection

  • Glycolic Facial

    Glycolic Facial prevents cell build-up on the skin, which causes a dull complexion, clogged pores and reduces acne breakouts and acne scars. This can give the skin a refreshed glow. Treatment done every week and lasts for 30 to 45 minutes.

  • Advanced Acne-Light Therapy

    A Light treatment that can be used to stimulate collagen production and kill acne causing bacteria on the skin, improving skin clarity- with no pain and no downtime and without harming skin. Results are often seen after just one treatment.

  • Acne Laser Treatment

    Effectively treat acne prone skin without damaging the surrounding tissue. It works by directing rays of concentrated light into the sebaceous glands and decreases the skin’s ability to produce oil, whilst killing the bacteria trapped in closed pores. Each treatment takes 30 minutes. Results are gradually faded after One treatment.

  • Microneedling Treatment

    Microneedling is a minimally invasive skin-rejuvenation procedure that involves the use of a device that contains fine needles. The needles are used to puncture the skin to create a controlled skin injury. Each puncture creates a channel that triggers the body to fill these microscopic wounds by producing new collagen and elastin. Through the process of neovascularization and neocollagenesis, there is improvement in skin texture and firmness, as well as reduction in scars, pore size, and stretch marks. These channels also improve the penetration of topical medicines about 1000 times thereby, allowing a faster and more efficient way to stimulate skin renewal and making the skin appear fresher and younger looking.
